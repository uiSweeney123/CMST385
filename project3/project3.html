The King of Pop

\n\n \n\n

\n Michael Jackson (1958–2009) was one of the most influential artists in modern music history.\n He began his career as a child with The Jackson 5 and later became a global superstar as a solo artist.\n Known for his groundbreaking music videos, signature dance moves, and record-setting albums,\n Jackson helped shape pop culture worldwide.\n

\n\n

\n His work combined pop, R&B, rock, funk, and soul into a sound that reached massive audiences.\n Beyond music, he influenced fashion, choreography, and visual storytelling in media.\n This fan site highlights a few major albums and recent news related to his legacy.\n
