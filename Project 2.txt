    <h2>Project Concept</h2>

    <h3>Website Overview</h3>
    <p>
        This project involves the creation of a <strong>personal portfolio website</strong> designed to showcase my
        skills, projects, and professional background in <em>web and digital design</em>.
        The website will act as a centralized hub for employers, instructors, and collaborators to learn more about my work.
    </p>

    <h4>Goals and Objectives</h4>
    <p>
        The primary objectives are to <u>present my work professionally</u>, improve usability through clean navigation,
        and demonstrate foundational HTML and CSS skills.
    </p>

    <h4>Target Audience</h4>
    <ul>
        <li>Potential employers</li>
        <li>Professors and classmates</li>
        <li>Creative collaborators</li>
    </ul>

    <h4>Planned Features</h4>
    <ol>
        <li>Responsive layout</li>
        <li>Project gallery</li>
        <li>Contact form (non-functional)</li>
    </ol>

    <!-- SECTION 2 -->
    <h2>Site Map</h2>

    <h3>Site Organization</h3>
    <p>
        The website will be organized into five main pages with a consistent navigation bar.
        Each page will focus on a specific area of content while maintaining a unified visual design.
    </p>

    <h4>Planned Pages</h4>
    <ul>
        <li>Home</li>
        <li>About Me</li>
        <li>Projects</li>
        <li>Resume</li>
        <li>Contact</li>
    </ul>
